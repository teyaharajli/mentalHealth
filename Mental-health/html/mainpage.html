We are a non-profit organisation aiming for a better country, and a better world. That's why we operate several programs and services like the 24/7 online chat, and many other programs.On average, every two days in lebanon someone loses his life Due to suicide and life stress; and an average of 6 people every 6 hours ending their lives. Because most people find it hard, or mostly impossible to tak about their weakness, taboos, and their thoughts, this program helps them to reach support anonymously. And because "Talking lengthens life-time" our volunteers are available 24/7 for immediate emotional support, embracing and organising thoughts.
